The Earth is Us: Love Letters to the Earth

Call out for contributions to webzine

'The Earth is not just our environment. We are the Earth and the Earth is us. We have always been One with the Earth.'

Inspired by Thich Nhat Hanh’s ‘Love Letters to the Earth’, I am putting together a collective webzine for World Earth Day 2025, and invite you to contribute.

Send me your own ‘Love letter to the Earth’ in any form—artwork, stories, poems, songs, soundscapes, videos, recordings—anything that expresses your connection with our planet.

The zine will be landscape format, but you can submit in any dimensions or file format.

All contributions will be compiled into an interactive webzine and published online on April 22nd, World Earth Day.

Send your contributions to Jean at theearthisus0@gmail.com by Monday, April 11th.

Questions? Reach out via the email above. Looking forward to your contributions!

Jean
